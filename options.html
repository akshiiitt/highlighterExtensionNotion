Text Highlighter & Notion Saver - Setup

Step 1: Create Notion Integration

1. Go to notion.so/my-integrations

2. Click "New integration" and give it a name

3. Copy the "Internal Integration Token" (starts with secret_)

Step 2: Prepare Notion Page

1. Create or open a Notion page where highlights will be saved

2. Click "Share" → "Invite" → Select your integration

3. Copy the page ID from the URL (32 characters, with or without dashes)

Step 3: Configure Extension

How to Use

1. Select any text on a webpage

2. Right-click → "Highlight & Save to Notion"

3. Text gets highlighted and saved to your Notion page!
